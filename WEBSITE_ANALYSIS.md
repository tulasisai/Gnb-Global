# GNB Global Website - Deep Analysis & Improvement Report
## Prepared: February 9, 2026

---

## 1. CURRENT WEBSITE OVERVIEW
**Website:** https://gnb.group/  
**Business:** Tension fabric buildings manufacturer (US & Canada)  
**Key Products:** Spartan, Centurion, Custom fabric buildings

---

## 2. CURRENT STRENGTHS
✅ **Good Product Content**
- Clear descriptions of Spartan, Centurion, and Custom buildings
- Specific sizing information (32-100 ft, 40-150+ ft, up to 400 ft)

✅ **Trust Elements**
- Partner logos (Tesla, Rivian, USAF, etc.)
- Safety certifications listed
- Warranty information ("Give A Sh!t Guarantee")

✅ **Comprehensive Resources**
- Blog/GNB Learn section
- Related products/companies (GNB Doors, RWES, Supreme Tarps)
- Multiple contact options

✅ **Brand Personality**
- Unique voice ("Give A Sh!t Guarantee")
- Clear value proposition

---

## 3. CRITICAL ISSUES TO FIX

### ❌ **Visual Design Problems**
1. **Cluttered Layout** - Too much content competing for attention
2. **Poor Visual Hierarchy** - Unclear what users should focus on first
3. **Inconsistent Spacing** - Sections feel cramped
4. **Color Inconsistency** - Need clearer brand color system
5. **Typography Issues** - Heading sizes not properly differentiated

### ❌ **Navigation Issues**
1. **Confusing Information Architecture** - Multiple product types not clearly distinguished
2. **Weak Call-to-Action** - "Get Quote" button not prominent enough
3. **Hard to Find Services** - Service descriptions buried in content
4. **No Clear Product Comparison** - Difficult to compare building types

### ❌ **User Experience Problems**
1. **Mobile Responsiveness** - Unclear how well it adapts to mobile
2. **Content Overload** - Too much information at once
3. **Weak Hero Section** - Doesn't immediately convey value
4. **Poor Form Optimization** - Quote request form not strategically placed
5. **Low Visual Appeal** - Needs more professional imagery/graphics

### ❌ **Performance & Credibility**
1. **Related Companies Buried** - GNB Doors, RWES, Supreme Tarps are hidden
2. **Testimonials Missing** - No client success stories
3. **Process Not Explained** - How buying works is unclear
4. **Geographic Presence Unclear** - US/Canada locations not highlighted

## 7. COLOR & DESIGN SYSTEM

**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Blue: #1a3a52 (trust, professionalism)
- Industrial Orange: #f57d1a (energy, action)
- Accent Gray: #6b7280 (text, secondary)
- Clean White: #ffffff (background)

**Typography:**
- Headings: Bold, 28-48px (depending on level)
- Body: 16px, line-height 1.6
- Emphasis: Medium weight for CTAs

**Spacing:**
- Section padding: 80px (desktop), 40px (mobile)
- Card spacing: 2rem
- Line height: 1.6-1.8 for readability
