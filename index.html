Find answers to common questions about our fabric buildings

A tension fabric building is a large clear-span structure featuring galvanized carbon steel frame trusses covered with engineered fabric membranes like GNB's NovaShield¬Æ II HDPE. These buildings offer quick installation, lower operational costs, energy savings, naturally bright interiors, flexible foundation options, and superior corrosion resistance. GNB Global manufactures buildings ranging from 32 feet to over 400 feet wide for commercial, industrial, military, and public sector applications.

Fabric buildings can be installed in weeks rather than months. Components are prefabricated off-site, shipped to your location, and assembled rapidly by our certified installation crews. GNB Global's safety-focused teams conduct daily briefings and maintain certifications including Avetta, ISNetworld, CSA A660, and COR, allowing us to work on North America's most rigorous job sites. Exact timelines depend on building size and site conditions.

GNB Global buildings are backed by our "Give A Sh!t Guarantee" ‚Äì a comprehensive 16-year warranty on fabric covers. With proper maintenance, fabric membranes typically last 20-30+ years, while galvanized steel frame components are designed for 40+ years of service. Our 5-Year Preventative Maintenance Program includes rigorous annual inspections, 25% service discounts, and immediate repairs to anchorage and fabric issues for maximum longevity.
